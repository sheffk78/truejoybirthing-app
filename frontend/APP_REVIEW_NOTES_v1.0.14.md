True Joy Birthing supports three roles: Mom, Doula, and Midwife.

The Mom role is free — Moms do not subscribe. Pro subscriptions
(`truejoy.pro.monthly` $29.99/month and `truejoy.pro.annual` $274.99/year,
both with a 14-day free trial) are for care providers (Doulas and
Midwives) only.

To reach the paywall and the "Start 14-Day Free Trial" button:

1. Launch the app and log in with the Midwife demo account below.
2. Tap the Profile tab (bottom right).
3. Tap "Manage Subscription" (or any Pro upsell prompt).
4. Tap a plan card (Monthly or Annual).
5. Tap "Start 14-Day Free Trial" — the native Apple purchase sheet appears.

Demo accounts:

- Midwife: demo.midwife@truejoybirthing.com / DemoMidwife2024!
- Doula:   demo.doula@truejoybirthing.com   / DemoDoula2024!
- Mom (free, no subscription): demo.mom@truejoybirthing.com / DemoMom2024!

Both subscription products are attached to this version under "In-App
Purchases and Subscriptions". App Review screenshots and per-product
Review Notes are uploaded on each product page. The Paid Apps Agreement
is Active under the Account Holder.

The app supports iPad. Recommended test device: iPad Air 11" (M3) running
iPadOS 26.4.1 or later.

In-app messaging exists between Moms and their connected providers
(Doulas, Midwives). This is reflected in the Age Rating answers
(Messaging and Chat = Yes).
